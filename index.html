Fashion

EcoStyle Studio is a men's and women's wear boutique based in Seattle. We were the first in Washington State to offer a range of environmentally friendly fashion
All the collections found at our boutique and online are beautiful, stylish, desirable and environmentally friendly

Louisa Huff, Designer

EcoStyle Studio is owned by Louisa Huff. She was born in France to hard working clothiers, Ramond and Elsa Huff
At a young age, Louisa worked in her parent's business where she fell in love with the fashion, the fabrics, the style, and the energy of the fashion industry. She continues to pour this passion into EcoStyle Studio and its products

Environmentally Friendly

We are committed to improving sustainability in the fashion industry and, this year we were awarded the Sustainable Fashion of the Year by EcoVision
